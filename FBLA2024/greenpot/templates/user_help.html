Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How to get started?

A: Click on the partners tab on the navigation bar. From there you will see a page with your partners.

Q: Why do some partners not have any action features?

A: This is because some partners were not created by you, but other users. You may not modify those, but you can view them by clicking on the partner.

Q: How do the search filters and search bar work?

A: The checkboxes will take filter based on the Type of partner. This field can be only "Business" or "Community." The search bar will filter using a type ahead filter based on the Partner Name. As you type in the bar, the partners will filter on your screen.

Q: What does the "Generate Report" button do?

A: This button allows you to download a report of your partners. When the button is clicked you are redirected to a preview page with all the partners that were visible on your screen just before you were redirected.
